About US

NextAbility operates as a direct peer-to-peer (P2P) infrastructure for the exchange, sale, and donation of medical and mobility rehabilitation equipment. The platform functions strictly as a digital connector between individuals requiring therapeutic devices and the current owners. By eliminating commercial intermediaries and onsite payment processing, this operational model reduces end-user acquisition costs and extends the functional lifecycle of viable medical hardware through systematic redistribution.
